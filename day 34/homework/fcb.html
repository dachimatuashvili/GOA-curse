History

FC Barcelona, founded in 1899, is one of the most prestigious football clubs in the world. Known for their iconic style of play, they have won numerous domestic and international titles.

Stadium

The Camp Nou is the home of FC Barcelona. It is the largest stadium in Europe, with a capacity of over 99,000 spectators.
