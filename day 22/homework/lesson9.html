My Favourite Foods

Khinkali

Khinkali is a traditional Georgian dumpling filled with minced meat and spices. It's a delicious and hearty dish, often enjoyed with a touch of black pepper and consumed in one bite.

Khachapuri

Khachapuri is a Georgian dish consisting of a bread filled with cheese, eggs, and butter. It’s a popular comfort food, often served hot, straight from the oven.

Mtsvadi

Mtsvadi is Georgian-style skewered and grilled meat, typically pork or beef, seasoned with herbs and spices. It's a must-try for anyone craving smoky, juicy grilled meat.

Pizza

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy. It consists of a dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables, meats, and herbs.

Lobiani

Lobiani is a traditional Georgian bread stuffed with beans and spices. It’s a savory, filling dish that’s perfect for any time of the day.
